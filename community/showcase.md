## Featured Projects

### 🧾 InvoiceFlow

**Builder:** Sarah M. — freelance graphic designer

**Description:** A dead-simple invoicing app for freelancers who hate dealing with invoices. Create an invoice in under 60 seconds, send it to your client via email, and get notified when they view it. No sign-up required for clients. Sarah built this because she was tired of paying $30/month for bloated invoicing software when she just needed to send a clean PDF.

**Tools Used:** Next.js, Tailwind CSS, Supabase (database + auth), Resend (email), Vercel (hosting), Claude (AI coding assistant)

## 🌟 Want to Be Featured?

We'd love to showcase your project! Whether it's a polished SaaS or a scrappy weekend hack, if you built it with vibe coding, it belongs here.

### Submission Template

Copy the template below, fill it in, and submit it as a [pull request](https://github.com) or share it in our community:

```markdown
### [Emoji] App Name

**Builder:** [Your name] — [one-line about you]

**Description:** [2-4 sentences about what your app does, why you built it,

**Time to Build:** [How long it took you]

**Link:** [URL to your live app]

**Screenshot:**
![App Name Screenshot](url-to-your-screenshot.png)

**Biggest lesson learned:**
[One sentence about the most important thing you learned building this]
```

### Submission Guidelines

- **Be real.** Only submit apps you actually built. Hypothetical projects don't count.
- **Be kind.** This is a supportive community. Celebrate others' wins alongside your own.
- **Any size counts.** Your app doesn't need to have paying users or fancy features. A working prototype is worth celebrating.
- **Include a screenshot.** A picture is worth a thousand words. Show off your UI!
- **Share your story.** We care about the journey as much as the result. What was hard? What surprised you?

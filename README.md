## 👋 Who Is This For?

This playbook is for **you** if you are:

- 🧑‍💼 **A solopreneur** with a product idea but no engineering team
- 💡 **An indie hacker** who wants to build fast and ship faster
- 🏪 **A small business owner** who needs a custom app but can't afford a dev shop
- 🎨 **A designer** who wants to bring mockups to life without waiting on developers
- 📋 **A product manager** who wants to prototype ideas in hours, not sprints
- 🎓 **A complete beginner** who has never written a line of code — and that's perfectly fine

**You don't need to know how to code.** You need to know how to describe what you want. This playbook teaches you how to do that extraordinarily well, so AI builds it for you.

---

## 🧠 What You'll Learn

This playbook is organized into **7 parts** and **19 chapters** that take you from "What is code?" all the way to "I just launched my product."

| Part | What You'll Learn | Chapters |
|------|-------------------|----------|
| **1. Foundation** | What vibe coding is, which tools to use, and build your first app in 30 minutes | Ch 1–3 |
| **2. The Art of Prompting** | How to talk to AI effectively, turn ideas into specs, and give AI the context it needs | Ch 4–6 |
| **3. Building Your App** | Build features iteratively, use ready-made recipes, and debug like a pro (without coding) | Ch 7–9 |
| **4. Quality & Security** | Make sure your app works well and stays safe | Ch 10–11 |
| **5. Supercharge with MCP & Skills** | Connect AI to real-world tools, teach it new skills, and build your solopreneur workflow | Ch 12–14 |
| **6. Ship It!** | Deploy your app to the world and handle what comes after launch | Ch 15–16 |
| **7. Level Up** | Learn advanced patterns, think about the business side, and see what's coming next | Ch 17–19 |

## 🚀 Quick Start: Pick Your Path

Not sure where to begin? Pick the path that fits you best:

### 🌱 "I'm brand new to all of this"
**Start at [Chapter 1: What Is Vibe Coding?](chapters/01-what-is-vibe-coding/)**
You'll learn what this is all about, pick your tools, and build your first app in 30 minutes.

### 💡 "I have an idea but don't know where to start"
**Start at [Chapter 5: From Idea to PRD](chapters/05-idea-to-prd/)**
You'll learn how to turn a fuzzy idea into a clear spec that AI can build from.

### 🔨 "I've played around — now I want to build something real"
**Start at [Chapter 8: The Recipe Book](chapters/08-recipe-book/)**
Jump straight into building common app patterns: dashboards, SaaS tools, chatbots, and more.
